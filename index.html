'Never Cease'

The expression is shorthand for a family saying of mine which says that "those who dare to teach, must never cease to learn. From an early age I remember seeing these words on an award that my grandfather had as a paperweight. For me, these words ring the truest when I myself am reaching beyond what I am comfortable and striving to better myself. Having spent the past 13 years teaching in Title 1 school districts, I have benefited from the opertunities to continue my education both through formal classes and colaboration with students, faculty, and community stakeholders.

With a trackrecord of sucess, I'm ready to start listening to your hopes and dreams helping you to unpack the 'how' and 'why' of your goals for the future.

Recipe Roulette

Stuck wondering what to cook for your next culinary adventure? No idea what to snag at the local market for a trendy beer that will make you look like you know what 'flavor pairings' are? Well waffle no longer! Welcome to **Recipe Roulette** the only (okay, like, one of maybe several) recipe user interfaces that seeks to streamline your culinary experince. We hope you'll give us a spin.

Start off by selecting a random beer to explore - yes that's right, completely random from our database of beers that may, or probably wont, be at your local grocery store. From there, check out the flavors and foods that are recomended pairings. If nothing catches your eye, don't stop just yet - spin that wheel again and get another selection. Once you've found a product that catches your eye go ahead and check out our recipy finder that, along with your input, will call up top recipies from all around the web that match your query. So stop eating drivethrough that leaves you feeling like 'craps' and step up to roulette - RECIPE ROULETTE.

Password Generator

Here is a great tool for customizing the strength of your passwords. Use this application to generate truely random passwords from 8-128 characters that contain at least one of the following character values - lowercase letters, uppercase letters, numerical values 0-9, and special symbols. Don't worry, if you forget to add a character type, we'll not-so-gently remind you through the use of forced alerts to your screen! While we can't promise your family will be able to remember their passwords, we are confident that if you use this to generate a 10 digit phone number for tech support whoever you give it to will be very unlikely to reach you. Click on through to deffinately see the deployed site, and not some random image from Reddit. Promise.

Work Day Scheduler

Here is a simple tool that will help you stay organized during a busy day at work. At home, or in the office - so long as you have internet you can head over to Work Day Scheduler to plan your day. Even when you close the browser tab, your schedule will persist. No promises that you'll get through all those meetings though.
